MY GNI

Our Mission

Welcome to the MYGNI, a community-driven platform dedicated to providing valuable resources and information for students, alumni, and all those connected to GNI.

Our Purpose

We aim to create a hub for GuruNanak Institutions enthusiasts to share class notes, study materials, campus news, and insights on college life. This website is not affiliated with the GNI administration but is built by passionate students and alumni who want to support the college community.

Why We Exist

Our existence is motivated by a desire to assist students in their academic journey, connect alumni, and provide a space for sharing knowledge. We believe that together, we can make the College experience even more enriching.

Community-Driven

This website thrives on the contributions of our College students and alumni. We encourage active participation, whether through uploading class notes, engaging in discussions, or sharing your college stories. Let's build this community together.
